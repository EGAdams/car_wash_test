Why We Should Be Your REALTOR®

Thank you for visiting our website! Our goal is to provide you with the best home buying and home selling experience you have ever had. Our no pressure approach means everything on this site is free for you to use and you only have to worry about contacting us when you are ready or need some help!

Dan and I met when we both worked for a broker in Brooksville Florida in Hernando County.  I was the Administrative Assistant working on getting my license and he was an experienced agent.  Dan is not computer savvy and I had no experience.  We decided to get together to form “The Vision Team”.  Together with our love for people, we add a man’s and woman’s perspective which makes for a comfortable setting when buying or selling real estate.  We pride ourselves on being knowledgeable in Residential property, Commercial property, Vacant Land and New Construction.

With over thirty years of combined Real Estate experience and experiences with many of types of businesses and people, we are confident in what we do. In our ever changing world when it relates to laws and regulations you can rely on us to stay current.  Doing so helps us better guide you through the buying and selling process.  What drives us?  Our passion for Real Estate and helping people.  Our experience, knowledge, good work ethic and a good senses of humor is what we bring to the table.  Oh and one more thing, we answer our phones.

As active members of the community, we are better able to help you with some of the decisions needed to purchase or sell your property.

Feel free to call us today for an interview to get to know us better.  We look forward to it and we hope you enjoy our website.
